The Challenge

Education is a powerful tool that the Phoenix Library continually seeks to share with the community on a daily basis, yet needed a way to reach out younger minds in a refreshing manner.

The Solution

We captured the textures and colors of the beautiful building and compiled all the images in a interactive 3d brochure. Kids now have the opportunity to adventure even deeper into the library while they enjoyed reading their favorite books.
